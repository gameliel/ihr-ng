About Us

Welcome to IHR Established Since 2017

Initiative for improved health and Human Right was founded on 21st FEB 2017 by Victor Aweke and became registered on a later date with CAC. Prior to obtaining her certificate of incorporation, IHR provided community support services as it relates to legal community protection services, public education and promotion services, religious interpersonal sessions as prevention, Interpersonal disclosure and story session for People living with HIV/AIDs as prevention with positive. accommodation/roommate link services and other capacity building and prevention interventions.

Vision

A free society where the health needs of all persons are met and their fundamental Human rights are protected, respected, and reassured in Nigeria.

Mission

To train as to provide community support services and sustainability as it relates to health and human right for all persons especially for vulnerable and marginalized groups in Nigeria

Objectives

  1. To promote health and prevent Human Rights violations to safeguard the Fundamental Rights of every citizen and non-citizens in Nigeria.
  2. To educate and promote the health right as it relates to overall development & welfare of children, women in all vulnerable persons in Nigeria
  3. To create online forums, publish and distribute leaflets, brochures, and paper periodicals, to disseminate knowledge to build Trust and sense of responsibility in Nigeria.
  4. To promote and advocate for the Fundamental Freedom for all without any discrimination of race, religion, caste, color, sex, and language.
  5. To bring diplomatic and commercial pressures on regimes those violate human rights, to ensure that they respect the basic rights of their citizens and non-citizens.
  6. To study and prepare reports on the violation of human rights by the State and its agencies - the police and criminal justice system inconsistent with human rights norms.
  7. To provide legal aid and advice for the indigent and weaker sections of the society.
  8. To create an interface between the developed nations and Nigeria when pertaining to health and human right issues.
